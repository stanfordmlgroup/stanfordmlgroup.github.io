AI for Healthcare Research History

The AI for Healthcare (AIHC) work in the lab began through the AI for Healthcare Bootcamp which was founded in Fall 2017. The AIHC Bootcamp has now expanded into a new Harvard-Stanford joint program called the Medical AI Bootcamp.

Many of our AIHC research projects, including projects in the AIHC Bootcamp, have been in close collaboration with the Stanford Center for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine & Imaging (AIMI).
