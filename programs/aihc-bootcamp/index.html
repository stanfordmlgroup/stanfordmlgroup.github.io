Stanford ML Group

The 2017 - 2021 AI For Healthcare (AIHC) Bootcamp has now expanded into a new Harvard-Stanford joint program called the Medical AI Bootcamp.

AIHC Bootcamp History

Since its founding at Stanford in Fall 2017, the AIHC Bootcamp has graduated 100+ students with 15+ collaborating faculty at Stanford Medicine and Engineering, UCSF, and Harvard. Over half of the students had their first research experiences in the bootcamp. We are proud to share that our bootcamp alumni have pursued PhD programs at institutions including MIT, Stanford, UW; led industry efforts at Google Brain, Apple Health, Microsoft Research, Tesla AI; and started AI startups including Valar Labs and Wispr AI.
